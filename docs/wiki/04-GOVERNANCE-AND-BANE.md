# Governance & BANE — Enforcement Architecture

## Purpose

Define how system integrity, authority, and constraints are enforced.

---

## Governance Model

All actions must be:

- Authorized
- Logged
- Attributable

---

## BANE Responsibilities

### Constraint Enforcement
No unsafe or unauthorized execution.

### Audit Enforcement
Every action produces a trace.

### Intent Validation
All requests must pass motive validation.

---

## Rules

- No silent execution
- No authority override
- No unlogged action

---

## Principle

Governance is not optional. It is embedded in execution.
