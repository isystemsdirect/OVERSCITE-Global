# SCINGULAR™ Canonical Definitions

## Ecosystem Core
- **SCINGULAR™**: SCINGULAR™ is a human-governed, AI-augmented operational ecosystem that unifies cognition, workflow, hardware interaction, and domain intelligence into a single structured environment while preserving human authority.
- **SCING**: Scing is the IU-facing interface that translates human intent into governed system actions and maintains operational clarity across all system layers.
- **LARI-ArcHive™**: A super-advanced LARI engine responsible for system translation, lifecycle governance, manifest packaging, and cross-system continuity. (Downstream of Scing).
- **XSCITE™ Builder**: The OVERSCITE™ interface for designing and validating SCIMEGA™ systems.

## Authority & Identity
- **ARC (Authority, Responsibility, & Certification)**: ARC represents the accountable human identity layer binding all actions and authorizations to a verified individual.
- **IU Imprint Binding**: The unique cryptographic link between a human's intellectual presence (IU) and the system interface (Scing).

## Governance & Safety (BFI Stack)
- **BFI Autonomy**: BFI autonomy is a human-governed automation model controlled by Scing under BANE and TEON constraints.
- **BANE (Biological/Behavioral Audit & Network Enforcement)**: BANE enforces governance, validates intent, and blocks unsafe or unauthorized actions.
- **TEON (Temporal Execution & Orchestration Node)**: TEON enforces real-time safety constraints and overrides system behavior when safety boundaries are violated.
- **Pilot Interrupt**: A mandatory override mechanism that allows immediate human takeover through anchor hold transition.
- **Anchor Hold**: A stabilization state ensuring safe transition between autonomy and manual control.

## Execution & Boundaries
- **SCIMEGA™**: SCIMEGA™ is a hybrid operating system and hardware execution platform enabling governed autonomy and inspection operations.
- **PL Boundary (Physical Laboratory)**: The governed physical laboratory environment where hardware embodiment and device modeling are represented.
- **Dry-Link**: An activation-aware metadata-only synchronization boundary. Telemetry flows inward; no commands flow outward.
- **Reality Bridge**: A controlled boundary governing any future transition to real-world execution.

## Intelligence Layers
- **DocuSCRIBE™**: A professional document intelligence system enhanced by Scing Augmented Automation.
- **Methodology Stack**: A structured inspection workflow system binding methods, capabilities, and scheduling logic.
- **Weather Intelligence**: An environmental intelligence layer informing safety, scheduling, and operational readiness.
