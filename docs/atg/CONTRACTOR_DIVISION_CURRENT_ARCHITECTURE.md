# Contractor Division Current Architecture

## Overview
The Contractor Division manages the relationship between OVERSCITE™ mission orchestration and external service providers, including subcontractors, equipment vendors, and field specialists.

## Subcontractor Scheduling
SmartSCHEDULER™ incorporates subcontractor availability and certification status into mission planning. Subcontractors are bound by OVERSCITE™ governance while operating within a mission context.

## Proposal Intelligence
LARI engines provide analytical support for evaluating contractor proposals, comparing costs, capabilities, and past performance against mission requirements.

## Contract Workflows
Standardized workflows for contract initiation, execution, and closure. Every contract phase is BANE-governed and witness-recorded in ArcHive™.

## Method Packs for Contractors
Specific Inspection Method Packs are assigned to contractors based on their certified capabilities. Contractors can only execute methods they are trained and authorized for.

## DocuSCRIBE™ and Scing Augmented Automation Relationship
Contractors use DocuSCRIBE™ for field reporting. Scing Augmented Automation assists in aligning contractor findings with OVERSCITE™ reporting standards, ensuring consistency and compliance across the division.

## Authority & ARC Identity
All contractor personnel participating in a mission must be ARC-verified. Their actions are cryptographically bound to their identity, providing full accountability within the OVERSCITE™ ecosystem.
