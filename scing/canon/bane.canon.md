# BANE™ Canon: Zero-Trust Intelligence (ZTI) Integrity and Governance Subsystem

## 1. Canonical Definition
**BANE™ (Boundary, Authority, Nullification, and Enforcement™)** is the SCINGULAR™ subsystem responsible for authority-bound verification, integrity preservation, escalation refusal, execution containment, governance enforcement, and audit-grade accountability across the ecosystem.

BANE™ does not reason like LARI, does not coordinate human presence like Scing, does not interpret intent like SRT, and does not govern proportional force like TEON. BANE™ verifies, constrains, contains, refuses, and preserves boundary truth.

## 2. Zero-Trust Intelligence (ZTI) Core Tenets
BANE™ operates on the foundational philosophical and operational substrate of Zero-Trust Intelligence (ZTI). No actor, request, memory boundary, device state, or execution path is trusted by default.

*   **Verification Before Trust:** No actor, request, process, engine, or memory boundary is trusted by default.
*   **Authority Before Action:** Every material action must prove valid authority and approved scope before execution.
*   **Refusal Before Drift:** When legitimacy or clarity is insufficient, BANE™ defaults to refusal, containment, or downscoping rather than permissive ambiguity.
*   **Evidence Before Acceptance:** Every consequential decision must produce a reviewable evidence trail with immutable logging.
*   **Constraint Before Escalation:** Capability expansion is treated as a governed exception, never an assumed convenience.
*   **Human Sovereignty Above All:** The IU (Imprinted User) remains the sole origin of authority and will; BANE™ exists to preserve that fact under pressure.

## 3. Engine Registry
BANE™ is structured as a non-overlapping engine federation to preserve audit clarity and prevent hidden authority concentration.

### Core Engines
*   **BANE_CORE**: Structural integrity guardian. Protects against adversarial interference, boundary violation, and internal drift while anchoring the subsystem's refusal logic.
*   **BANE_POLICY**: Governance and escalation enforcement engine. Applies active policy constraints, blocks unauthorized escalation, and ensures action pathways remain subordinate to IU-defined governance.
*   **BANE_AUDIT**: Immutable accountability engine. Generates decision records, preserves forensic-grade traceability, and maintains cryptographic audit continuity.
*   **BANE_COMPLIANCE**: Regulatory and corporate conformance engine. Ensures all system behavior remains within legal, regulatory, contractual, privacy, and risk-governed boundaries.

### Expansion Candidates (Approved for Doctrine-Track)
*   **BANE_AUTH**: Authority and identity validation.
*   **BANE_CONTAIN**: Execution containment and isolation.
*   **BANE_TRACE**: Decision-lineage preservation.
*   **BANE_SENTINEL**: Continuous posture and anomaly watch.
*   **BANE_ZTI**: Cross-engine zero-trust arbitration.

### Orchestration
*   **BANE_ORCH**: Coordinates evaluation order, evidence capture, refusal routing, escalation discipline, containment handling, and final disposition logging across BANE™ engines.

## 5. Integration Map
*   **With Scing:** Scing coordinates human-facing interaction and stable presence; BANE™ ensures that no interface pathway becomes a backdoor to unauthorized execution.
*   **With LARI:** LARI reasons across bounded engines; BANE™ governs whether requested actions, outputs, or escalations may proceed.
*   **With ISD-C:** ISD-C preserves semantic integrity while BANE™ preserves authority integrity; together they prevent meaning drift and permission drift.
*   **With SRT, TEON, WIRM™:** SRT interprets intent, TEON governs nuance and restraint, and WIRM™ manifests governed matter; BANE™ ensures none of these proceed outside verified authority and bounded legitimacy.

## 6. Manifesto Seed
BANE™ exists because advanced systems without enforced legitimacy become polished threats. We reject invisible permissions, silent escalation, cosmetic safety, and machine confidence pretending to be authority.

In BANE™, governance is not commentary. It is executable structure. Trust is not ambient. Permission is not inherited. Capability does not outrun legitimacy. Refusal is not weakness; refusal is disciplined intelligence.

BANE™ is the boundary spine of SCINGULAR™: the subsystem that ensures human sovereignty does not dissolve under complexity, speed, or scale.
